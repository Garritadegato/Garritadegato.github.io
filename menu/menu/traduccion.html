About us



About Us?:


We are an online company called “cat garrita” we offer customers the opportunity to explore and purchase a
wide range of clothing, accessories and footwear from the comfort of their homes through an online
platform. In addition to presenting attractive products with detailed information on sizes, materials and garment care. Offering
secure payments, flexible return policies, and responsive customer service to ensure a satisfying
shopping experience.




Mission:


Provide high-quality fashion products, satisfy the needs and preferences of our customers, promote the sustainability
of online clothing sales. Likewise include design innovation, the promotion of self-expression through
fashion and the commitment to ethical business practices.




Vision:


Sell a product in which your personality can be reflected as well as the customer's tastes, and thus help them feel
what they use safely and comfortably, provide products that adapt to different needs and situations, with this we plan
to extend our scope of shipments to greater places and continue providing better
service.




Objective:

To consolidate its position as a leader in the national online fashion market, expanding its presence internationally and
diversifying its product offering to include not only clothing, but also accessories and personal care items. To
achieve this, the plan is to increase traffic to the online store through digital
marketing strategies such as social media advertising in order to increase sales by 20%. The
goal is to expand the customer base by creating a website for the store, offering a more convenient and attractive
shopping experience for customers, resulting in a 30% increase in online sales. This comprehensive approach will allow
the company to achieve its goals, positioning it as a benchmark in the competitive online fashion market.
